About Me

My name is Victoria Condos.
I am a woman in computing at Lockheed Martin Rotary and Missions Systems.
My current role is as a software intern where I serve remotely. In this position last summer, I worked on test automation. Also, I acted as project manager for my intern team as part of the Summer 2020 RMS Intern Project. As a result of this work, I have been invited to return to the internship program during the summer of 2021.
As a result of my success in my intern role, I have been asked to serve as an Intern Ambassador for the 2020-2021 academic year: an opportunity only offered to 20 of over 800 RMS Interns!
I am also the director of logistics for my sorority, Gamma Phi Beta, Zeta Sigma Chapter. In this position, I lead a team of women in organizing meetings between potential and active members during recruitment and tracking the votes from each round.
In fact, my hard work paid off when we successfully recruited the largest pledge class on campus in 2019 with 142 new members joining our sorority.
On a personal note, I enjoy spending my free time snowboarding, hiking, or reading!
